advayasiddhiḥom namaḥ śrī vajrasattvāya |prakṛti-prabhāsvaraṃ nāthaṃ sarvajñaṃ tribhavodbhavampraṇamya
śirasā vajramīṇsitārtha phalapradam |deśakāla tithivāra nakṣatraimaṇḍalaivināvakṣye'haṃ
vajrasattvasya saṃkṣepāt sādhanaṃ param

niyama vratopavāsai - rakṣaroccāraṇa-bhāvanaiḥatattva yogīna siddhayet kalpakoṭiśatairapi

viṇvajrodaka vījādyai rnāsikābhyantarod bhavaiḥpūjayet satataṃ mantrī ātmānaṃ tattva bhāvanaiḥ

jananī bhaginīścaiva duhitṛ bhāgineyikānprajñopāya vidhānena pūjayet yogavidsadā

ekāṅga vikalāṃ hīnāṃ śilpinīṃ śvapacikāṃ tathāyoṣitāṃ pūjayennityaṃ jñānavajraprabhāvanai

tatvasyemāni mantra vījapadāni bhavanti - om āḥ huṃyena yena hi vadhyante jantavo raudra karmaṇāsopāyena
tu tenaiva mucyante bhava vandhanāt

samayānaharahaḥ kuryāddivyāna pañcakulod bhavānpūjayecca pradīpādyaiḥ sakṣīrairviśvasambhavaiḥ

prātphulla nayano mantrī nityaṃ prahasitānanaḥcittamāropya saṃvodhau bhāvayeta jñānasāgaraṃ

yāvantaḥ sthirācalābhāvāḥ saṃtyatra tribhavālayesarvete tattvayogena draṣṭavyā vajradhṛk tathā

paravādinaśca ye kacilliṃga bhedai rvyavasthitātepyatra nāvamantavyā vajrasattva vikurvite

sarvān samarasīkṛtya bhāvānnairātmyaniḥsṛtānbhāvayet satataṃ mantrī dehaṃ prakṛta nirmalaṃ

gandhamālyādibhivastrai dhūpanaivedyake stathāgītavādyaistathā nṛtyai sopāyaibhajate vibhuḥ

na kaṣṭa kalpanāṃ kūryyānnopavāsaṃ na ca kriyāṃsnānaṃ śaucaṃ na caivātra grāma dharma vivarjanaṃ

na cāpi vandayedyevān kāṣṭha pāṣāṇa mṛṇmayānpūjā masyevakāyasya kūryānnitya samāhitaṃ

makṣikāchardi sammiśraiḥ viṇmūtrādyaiśca bhāvitaipañca pradīpa saṃyuktaiḥ pūjayedvajradhāriṇam

abalā svayaṃbhūkusumaiḥ sakṣīrai viśvasambhavaiḥpūjayedyevatāṃ tena dehasthāṃ tatva bhāvanaiḥ

parasva haraṇaṃ kuryāt paradārā niṣevanaṃvaktavyaṃ ca mṛṣāvākyaṃ sarvabuddhāṃścadhātayet

śaila mṛṇmaya caityādīn na kuryān pustakeratiṃna maṇḍalāni svapne'pi kāyavākcittakarmaṇā

jugupsānaiva kurvīta sarvavastuṣu mantravitvajrasattvaḥ svayaṃ tatra sākṣādrupeṇa saṃsthitaḥ

gamyāgamya vikalpaṃ tu bhakṣyā bhakṣyaṃ tathaivacapeyā peyaṃ tathā mantrī kuryyānaiva samāhitaḥ

vairocana samudbhūtān sarvaprāṇyeva saṃbhavān |prāṇavān guhyatatvajño bhokṣayet siddhihetunā

sarva varṇa samudbhutā jugupsā naiva yoṣitaḥsaiva bhagavatī prajñā saṃvṛtyā rūpamāśritā

nā tithirnaca nakṣatraṃ nopavāsovidhiyateadvaya jñāna yuktasya siddhirbhavati saugatī

vahunātra kimuktena yadbhavadupalabdhikaṃtatsarvaṃ tatvayogena draṣṭavyaṃ tatvavedinā

hastyaśva khara gāvoṣṭra pradīpaṃ śvānasambhavaṃmahāpradīpa sammiśra bhokṣayed yogavid sadā

na cādhyāsaktiṃ kurvīta ekasminnapi yogavitsamatā cittayogena bhāvanīyo bhavārṇṇavaḥ

utpatti sthiti nirodhañca asaṃpṛkta pṛthag janaiḥtasya bhāvetu saṃsāro nānyatra pralayodbhavaḥ

dinaṃ tu bhagavānvajrī naktaṃ prajñā vidhīyateevaṃ tu bhāvayed yogī laghusiddhimavāpnuyāt

yattadvyaktaṃ rūpaṃ tu sarvasattveṣu saṃsthitaṃguruvaktrāt paraṃtatvaṃ prāpyate nātra saṃśayaḥ

apratiṣṭhita nirvāṇaṃ ninirmittaṃ nirālayaṃvyāpakaṃ sarvasatveṣu saṃvodhi paramaṃ padaṃ

evaṃ matvā tu vai yogī yo bhāvayed vuddhimān sadāsa sidhyati na sandeho mandaṃ puṇyopi mānavaḥ

ācāryāt parataraṃ nāsti trailokya sacarācaraiyasya prasādāt prāpyante sidhyo'nekadhāvudhaiḥ

vajrasatvaḥ sarvaijñeyaḥ sarvavuddhai namaskṛtaḥācāryāḥ paramodevaḥ pūjanīyaḥ prayatnata

sa eva tathatārūpī lokānugraha hetunārūpamāśritya saṃvṛtyā saṃsthito yogapīṭhake

mṛtyureṣa vikalpoyaṃ na bhāvaḥ sarvavastuṣuhanyate savikalpena pṛthagjana vijṛmbhitaiḥ

śrī madoḍiyāna vinirgatā mahāyoga -pīṭhāgatā akhila yogatantra tatvagarbhā śrī lakṣmīmukha kamalādvinisṛtā
svādhiṣṭhān kramodayā sādhanopāyikā |||| samāpteti ||
